INTRODUCTION
Notion is an all-in-one productivity tool that helps to write notes, manage tasks, plan 
projects and collobrate, all in one place. Moreover, apart from this task, it will help you to create page and 
also host that page for free. Notion replaces Google docs, Excel, Trello and notes. 

USUAGE
It can be used by individuals and teams.

  1. Individuals: For personal knowledge management, habit tracking, or study organization.
  2. Teams: For internal documentation, Project management and team dashboards.

VARIOUS FEATURES OF NOTION:
  1. Flexible
  2. Project management
  3. Collaboration
  4. live pages
  5. Share and Access control
